"PropLit Consulting"

Welcome to RedBat Store At PropLit Consulting, we are more than just a consulting firm—we are catalysts for change, champions of growth, and advocates for empowerment. Our mission is to provide knowledge, purpose, and access, transforming lives and businesses through skills development, education support, and strategic business solutions. As a 100% black female-owned company, we are deeply committed to creating meaningful and lasting impact. Under the leadership of Sarah Mqadi, we have built a reputation for excellence in learning and skills development, business startup support, project management, and collaborative programs. With over 10 years of industry experience and a B-BBEE Level 1 certification, we equip individuals and organizations with the tools they need to succeed. From career development and financial literacy training to business compliance and funding proposals, we ensure our clients have the resources to thrive. We believe in the power of education, mentorship, and strategic planning to unlock opportunities and drive transformation. Whether you’re an entrepreneur looking to establish a business, a learner in need of academic support, or an organization seeking impactful training solutions, PropLit Consulting is your trusted partner for success.
